For our second semester exam we had to find a company, already consolidated on the market selling their products - and who has an insufficient online presence. The main task was to communicate our clients profile effectively by creating a strong, professional online presence to bring value to the client and their customers.

We worked with Gerner Jahncke - an independent high-end ceramic and glass studio. Beginning the project, we researched Gerner Jahncke's offering. Based on the research we formulated a coherent marketing strategy and a digital identity for the brand.

By the end of the project we’d helped them create social media content, made a high fidelity prototype to showcase our design - and a strategy for implementing both - taking into account business, marketing and product factors.
